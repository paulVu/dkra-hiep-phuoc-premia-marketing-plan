Giai doan 1

Mo nhan dien

Lam cho nguoi xem nho ten du an, nhan dien hinh anh, biet du an dang duoc trien khai bai ban.

  • Bai gioi thieu tong quan du an
  • Anh du an dep, dong bo brand
  • Video teaser ngan tu phim du an
  • Bai "vi sao nen biet den du an nay"

Source: Tong quan du an + Hinh anh du an + Phim du an

Giai doan 2

Dao sau thuyet phuc

Tra loi cau hoi cua khach, tao ly do quan tam, nang do nghiem tuc cua lead.

  • Bai phan tich thi truong/khu vuc
  • Bai ve mat bang/to roi/tai lieu
  • Bai chinh sach ban hang
  • Carousel anh + thong tin
  • Video voiceover hoac text motion

Source: Thong tin thi truong + Ke hoach kinh doanh + MKT tai lieu

Giai doan 3

Chot chuyen doi

Day khach vao inbox, tu van truc tiep hoac sales follow-up.

  • Bai CTA nhan full file / chinh sach
  • Bai phap ly tao an tam
  • Bai su kien / social proof
  • Reel recap
  • Bai "neu can tu van thi inbox ngay"

Source: Phap ly du an + Hinh anh su kien + Ke hoach kinh doanh

2. Doi tuong

3 nhom khach hang chinh

Nguoi mua de o

Quan tam: chat luong song, quy hoach, hinh anh du an, cam giac an tam, logic an cu lau dai.

  • Hook: "Du an nay danh cho ai muon o that?"
  • Noi dung manh: tong quan, hinh anh, mat bang
  • Kenh tot: Facebook, carousel, video ngoi nha

Nha dau tu

Quan tam: thong tin thi truong, khu vuc, ha tang, kha nang khai thac, thoi diem xuong tien.

  • Hook: "Vi sao thoi diem nay dang theo doi?"
  • Noi dung manh: thi truong, chinh sach, phap ly
  • Kenh tot: Facebook, Zalo group, tu van 1-1

Sales / moi gioi

Quan tam: do de tu van — bo tai lieu ro, hinh anh dep, phap ly du, policy de noi lai cho khach.

  • Hook: "Bo cong cu de tu van nhanh hon"
  • Noi dung manh: to roi, standee, chinh sach, phap ly
  • Kenh tot: Zalo noi bo, email, file PDF

6. Chien luoc kenh

Ma tran noi dung x kenh x muc tieu

Facebook / Fanpage

Kenh chinh de xay nhan dien va keo inbox.

  • Noi dung: Tat ca 6 tru cot
  • Format: Carousel, anh don, video
  • Tan suat: 1-2 bai/ngay
  • CTA: Inbox nhan file, nhan phap ly, nhan chinh sach
  • Anh goi y: 1-14.jpg, 18 chieu toi.jpg, mat bang

Reels / TikTok / Video ngan

Kenh de dung scroll va tao cam xuc nhanh.

  • Noi dung: Teaser, highlight, text motion
  • Format: 9:16, 15-30 giay
  • Tan suat: 3-4 video/tuan
  • CTA: Comment/inbox de lay bo tai lieu
  • Source: Cat tu Film thi truong.mp4

Zalo / Sales Enablement

Kenh noi bo hoac nhom sales de thong nhat cach tu van.

  • Noi dung: To roi, standee, chinh sach, phap ly
  • Format: PDF, anh, file doc
  • Muc tieu: Sales noi cung mot thong diep
  • CTA: Dung lam file gui khach 1-1
  • Source: MKT tai lieu & thiet ke

8. Checklist

Checklist trien khai tung tuan

Truoc khi bat dau (1 lan)

  • Doc ky Tong quan du an PDF — ghi lai 5 key facts
  • Doc Thong tin thi truong PDF — ghi lai 3 goc thi truong
  • Doc Chinh sach ban hang PDF — ghi lai cac diem chinh
  • Kiem tra folder Phap ly — ghi lai so giay phep
  • Tai anh du an — phan loai: ban ngay, chieu toi, dem
  • Tai phim du an — cat thanh 3-5 doan ngan
  • Tai logo/brand guideline — tao template overlay
  • Tao file theo doi: bai da dang, engagement, inbox

Moi tuan (lap lai)

  • Chuan bi 7-10 bai theo lich noi dung
  • Viet caption dung mau — dien thong tin tu source
  • Chon anh/video phu hop voi loai bai
  • Check: khong co claim tu bua (dau tu, gia, uu dai)
  • Check: brand dong bo (logo, mau sac, font)
  • Len lich dang (sang 8-9h hoac toi 7-9h)
  • Theo doi engagement — reply inbox trong 30 phut
  • Ghi nhan so inbox, so nguoi xin file, so lead
9. KPI

Chi so do luong theo giai doan

Tuan 1

Nhan dien

  • Reach tong: target 5,000+
  • Video views: target 1,000+
  • Profile visits: tang 30%
  • Saves/shares: 50+
Tuan 2-3

Niem tin & quan tam

  • Carousel completion rate: 60%+
  • Avg time on post: tang 20%
  • So nguoi hoi phap ly: 10+
  • So nguoi xin file tong quan: 20+
Tuan 4

Chuyen doi

  • So inbox: target 30+
  • So nguoi xin file chinh sach: 15+
  • So lead ban giao cho sales: 10+
  • So hen tu van: 5+
10. Quy tac

Quy tac bat buoc khi trien khai

KHONG duoc lam

  • Tu bua claim dau tu, tang gia, loi nhuan
  • Tu them uu dai, chiet khau, ho tro ngoai tai lieu
  • Dung hinh anh khong chinh thuc khi da co anh official
  • Phat bieu phap ly khi chua doi chieu folder phap ly
  • Phong dai quy mo, tien ich, hoac tien do

PHAI lam

  • Lay thong tin tu bo source chinh thuc
  • Dong bo brand (logo, mau sac, font)
  • Moi bai phai gan voi 1 muc tieu: nhan dien, tin tuong, hoac chuyen doi
  • Uu tien tu ngu chinh thuc tu tai lieu
  • Reply inbox trong 30 phut
